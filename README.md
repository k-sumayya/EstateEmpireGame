Core Gameplay Loop
Farm
↓
Earn Gold
↓
Build House
↓
Get Villager
↓
Villager Works
↓
Earn More Gold
↓
Build More Structures
↓
Grow Village

This is the loop that drives the entire game.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Stage 1: Manual Farming

Initially:

Player clicks a farm plot.

Harvest Wheat
+5 Gold
+2 Food

or

Cut Tree
+10 Wood

or

Mine Stone
+5 Stone

Everything is manual.

The player feels:

"I need resources."

This creates motivation.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Stage 2: First House

Requirements:

Wood = 100
Stone = 50
Gold = 200

Player builds:

🏠 Small Hut

Benefits:

Population +1

A villager arrives.

Now the player is no longer alone.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Stage 3: Villagers Work

Instead of the player farming forever:

Assign villager.

Villager #1
Task: Wheat Farm

Every minute:

+5 Wheat

Now the game becomes semi-automatic.

The player starts managing workers.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Stage 4: Expand Production

New buildings unlock.

Farm

Produces food.

Lumber Mill

Produces wood.

Quarry

Produces stone.

Village now looks like:

Farm
House
Lumber Mill
Quarry

The player becomes a manager rather than a laborer.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Stage 5: Village Growth

Population increases.

1 House = 1 Villager

5 Houses = 5 Villagers

20 Houses = 20 Villagers

More villagers mean:

More workers
More taxes
More production

Now growth becomes exponential.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Stage 6: Village Center

Unlock:

🏛 Village Hall

This becomes the heart of the settlement.

Upgrading it unlocks:

Village Level 2
Village Level 3
Village Level 4

Each level unlocks new buildings.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Stage 7: Economy

Introduce money generation.

Example:

Farm → Wheat

Market buys Wheat

Gold generated

Flow:

Food
↓
Trade
↓
Gold
↓
Construction

Now the economy feels realistic.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Stage 8: Town Era

Requirements:

Population 50

Village Hall Level 5

Gold 10,000

Unlock:

🏪 Market

🏫 School

⚒ Blacksmith

🏥 Clinic

The village officially becomes a town.
